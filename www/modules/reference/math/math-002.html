GEOMETRIC ANGLES



  • A right angle is
    exactly 90°

  • An acute angle is
    less than 90°

  • An obtuse angle is
    greater than 90°

  • A straight angle is
    exactly 180°


  • Complementary angles add up to 90°

  • Supplementary angles add up to 180°

  • One complete angle of rotation = 360°

GEOMETRIC CONGRUENCY CASES



  • S.S.S. POSTULATE

    Side-Side-Side
    ΔABC ≅ ΔDEF

  • S.A.S. POSTULATE

    Side-Angle-Side
    ΔABC = ΔKLM

  • A.S.A. POSTULATE

    Angle-Side-Angle
    ΔABC ≅ ΔPRS

  • H.S. POSTULATE

    Hypotenuse-Side
    ΔABC ≅ ΔXYZ


  • EQUILATERAL TRIANGLE

    3 Sides of Equal Length
    3 Angles of 60° Each

  • ISOSCELES TRIANGLE

    2 Sides of Equal Length
    2 Angles of 60° Each

  • SCALENE TRIANGLE

    3 Unequal Sides
    3 Unequal Angles
